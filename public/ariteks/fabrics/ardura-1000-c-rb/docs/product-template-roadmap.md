  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/ardura-1000-c-rb/images/ardura-1000-c-rb-main-pic-m__a8998f5db2.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- ArDura 1000 C Rb is heavyweight 100% polyamide 6.6 ripstop woven fabric for military equipment.
- The Pa6.6 high tenacity fibers gives for extra protection for military and police bags.
- The fabric has water and oil repellent function.
- It's used mostly for application which requires ultra durability.
- The antibacterial function is very usefull to eliminate all kind of odor formation during work.
