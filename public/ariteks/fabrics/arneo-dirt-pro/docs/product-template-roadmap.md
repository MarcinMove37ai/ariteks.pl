- Opis z title: `Water and Oil Repellent High Visible Warp Knit Fabric`
- Linia specyfikacji: `150 g/m², 100% Polyester (PES), Water and Oil Repellent High Visible Warp Knit Fabric`

### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- Water and Oil Repellent Fluorescent Warp Knit Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arneo-dirt-pro/images/arneo-dirt-pro-main-pic-m__5add6eb7bb.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- ArNeo Dirt Pro is 100% polyester, 150 g/m² warp knitted neon colored fabric for safety jackets with extra water and oil repellent function.
- EN ISO 20471 certified.
- Neon dyed fabrics they attract more attention than other colors. They absorb uv light and reflect as a visible light.
- As a result they shine more than other objects.
- Water and oil repellent function.(EN ISO 14419)
