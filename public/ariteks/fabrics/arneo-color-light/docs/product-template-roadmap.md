## 2. Dane z podstrony produktu

- Nazwa handlowa: **ArNeo Color Light**
- Opis z title: `Bias Fabric`
- Linia specyfikacji: `70 g/m², 100% Polyester, Knit Fabric`

### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- Fluorescent Warp Knit Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arneo-color-light/images/arneo-color-light-main-pic-m__a053e402ab.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- ArNeo Color Light is 100% polyester, 65-70 g/m² warp knitted bias tape.
- It used as bias farbic for safety jackets.
- Depending on your bias design, it is possible to deliver bias tapes from 1cm up to 12cm.
