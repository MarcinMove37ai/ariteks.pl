## 2. Dane z podstrony produktu

- Nazwa handlowa: **ArBir 200**
- Opis z title: `Knit Fabric for Safety Clothes`
- Linia specyfikacji: `220 g/m², 100% Polyester, High Visible, Woven Like Knit Fabric`

### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- Flat Surface Warp Knit Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arbir-200/images/arbir-200-main-pic-m__65072819fe.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- %100 polyester, 220 g/m², developed for jackets, raincoats and reflective strips base
- EN ISO 20471 certified.
- Neon dyed fabrics they attract more attention than other colors. They absorb uv light and reflect as a visible light.
- As a result they shine more than other objects.
