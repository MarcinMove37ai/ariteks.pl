## 2. Dane z podstrony produktu

- Nazwa handlowa: **ArDoku**
- Opis z title: `84% Polyester 10% Pu 6% Elastane softshell fabric`
- Linia specyfikacji: `300 g/m², 84% Polyester 10% Pu 6% Elastane, 3 layer laminated, Softshell Fabric`

### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- Softshell Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/ardoku/images/ardoku-main-pic-m__960fba1778.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- ArDoku is 84% polyester 10% pu 6% elastane softshell fabric. The top layer is elastic woven fabric, the middle layer is breathable tpu membrane, and the last layer is polar fabric.
- The basic usage is for winter jackets.
- ArDoku is certified for EN 343 and EN 20471
