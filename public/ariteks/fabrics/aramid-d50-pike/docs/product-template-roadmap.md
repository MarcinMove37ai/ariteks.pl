### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- 93% m-Aramid 5% p-Aramid 2% Carbon Flame Retardant Antistatic Pique Knit Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/aramid-d50-pike/images/aramid-d50-pike-main-pic-m__5de35e11f3.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- Antistatic Pique Knit Fabric 93% m-Aramid 5% p-Aramid 2% Carbon, inherent flame retardant antistatic pique knit fabric. Aramid d50 Pike fabric is universal flame retardant knit fabric for many applications.
- Aramid D50 Pike is inherent flame retardant with EN 11612 certification.
- Suitable for antistatic applications, certified for EN ISO 1149.
- It provides arc flash protection with EN 61482 class 1 (4 kA) certification.
