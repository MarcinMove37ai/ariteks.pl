  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arof-mesh-2-2/images/arof-mesh-2-2-main-pic-m__f70bc97a02.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- Arof Mesh 2*2 is 100% polyester, 50-60 g/m² warp knitted 2*2 mesh fabric.
- Due to mesh structure it can provide both safety for the material and transparency.
- It can be used as a bag's side pocket, parka interlining, raincoat back fabric and car seat's back cover.
