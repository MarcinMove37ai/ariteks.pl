## 2. Dane z podstrony produktu

- Nazwa handlowa: **ArGiyim Dura**

- Linia specyfikacji: `245 g/m², 50% Pa 6.6 50% Cotton, Water Repellent, Antibacterial Woven Fabric`

### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- Pa 6.6 Woven
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/argiyim-dura/images/argiyim-dura-main-pic-m__592bc1d508.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- ArGiyim Dura is 50% polyamide 6.6 50% Cotton woven fabric for military, police and gendarme wear. It is suitable for mobile motobikers police wear. The Pa6.6 high tenacity fibers gives for extra protection for polices. The front side of the fabric is polyamide 6.6 and the back side is cotton. The fabric has water and oil repellent function.
- It's used mostly for application which requires ultra durability.
- The antibacterial funtion is very usefull to eliminate all kind of odor formation during work.
- It is EN 20471 certified.
