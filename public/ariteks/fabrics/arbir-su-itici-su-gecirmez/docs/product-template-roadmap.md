- Nazwa handlowa: **ArBir Su İtici/Su Geçirmez**
- Opis z title: `Waterproof Knit Raincoat Fabric`
- Linia specyfikacji: `160 g/m², 75% Polyester 25% Polyurethane, High Visible, Waterproof, Woven Like Knit Fabric`

### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- Flat Surface Warp Knit Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arbir-su-itici-su-gecirmez/images/arbir-su-itici-su-gecirmez-main-pic-m__fb0d5ec61e.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- %100 polyester, 160 g/m², waterproof knit fabric developed for Jackets and Raincoats
- EN ISO 20471 certified.
- Neon dyed fabrics they attract more attention than other colors. They absorb uv light and reflect as a visible light.
- As a result they shine more than other objects.
- Waterproof function.(TS EN ISO 343)
