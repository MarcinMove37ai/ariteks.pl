### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- 80% Cotton 20% Stainless Steel EMK Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arshirt-emk/images/arshirt-emk-main-pic-m__2eacd30921.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- ArShirt EMK fabrics can be used as emi shileding for walls and baby cloth by reducing all kind of elecrectromagneic waves.
- Another application is conductive clothing for live line workers, anti-radar applications for military cages, ani-stress, and pain reducer bandages.
- ArShirt EMK is certfied as 60895 for line workers clothing.
