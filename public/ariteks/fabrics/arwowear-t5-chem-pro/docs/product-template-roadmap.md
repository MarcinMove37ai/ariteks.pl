### 2.2. Opis

- ArWoWear T5 Chem Pro is light weight 245 g/m², vat dyed, chemical resistant,65% polyester 35% cotton merserized workwear fabric.
- It is usefull for outdoor summer hard working conditions.
- Water and oil repellent (TS EN ISO 4920) function.
- Resistant to most industrial chemicals. (EN ISO 13034)
