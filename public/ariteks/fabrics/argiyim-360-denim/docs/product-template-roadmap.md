- Opis z title: `50% Polyester 50% Cotton Denim Fabric`
- Linia specyfikacji: `360 g/m², 50% Polyester 50% Cotton, Denim Woven Fabric`

### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- 50% Polyester 50% Cotton Denim Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/argiyim-360-denim/images/argiyim-360-denim-main-pic-m__275019f34a.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- 50% Polyester 50% Cotton denim twill fabric. The front side of the fabric is polyester which is ultra durable for work conditions and light, the back side of the fabric is cotton which provides breathability to the user.
- ArGiyim 360 Denim are used for pants and trousers.
