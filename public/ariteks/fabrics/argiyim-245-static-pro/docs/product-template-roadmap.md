### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- 80% Polyester 20% Cotton Polycotton twill Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/argiyim-245-static-pro/images/argiyim-245-static-pro-main-pic-m__7092bfd80f.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- 79% Polyester 20% Cotton 1% Carbon PolyCotton antistatic, chemical resistant twill fabric. The front side of the fabric is 100% Polyester which is ultra durable for work conditions and light, the back side of the fabric is cotton which provides breathability to the user.
- ArGiyim²45 Static Pro are used in pans and trousers in clean rooms.
- Certified for EN ISO 1149-5(1149-3 Method 3) and for EN 13034
