### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

### 2.2. Opis

- It's used for lightweight outdoor roof systems.
- The base material is polyester and elastane mixture knit fabric which is coated by polyurethane layer.
- It's three time more flexible than other tarpaulins and do not contain any pvc.
