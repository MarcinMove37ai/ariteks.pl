## 2. Dane z podstrony produktu

- Nazwa handlowa: **ArDura 500 Fr**
- Opis z title: `Pa6.6 woven flame retardant coated fabric for Police, Firefighters and Gendarme wear`
- Linia specyfikacji: `240 g/m², 95% Pa 6.6 5%Pu, Water Repellent, Antibacterial, Waterproof, Flame Retardant Woven Fabric`

### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- Pa 6.6 Woven
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/ardura-500-fr/images/ardura-500-fr-main-pic-m__38f46a15ad.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- ArDura 500 Fr is lightweight 95% polyamide 6.6 5% polyurethane woven fabric for military, police and gendarme wear. It is suitable for mobile motobikers police wear.
- The Pa6.6 high tenacity fibers gives for extra protection for polices.
- The fabric has water and oil repellent function.
- It's used mostly for application which requires ultra durability.
- The antibacterial function is very usefull to eliminate all kind of odor formation during work.
- The fabric has flame retardant coating on back side which gives flame retardant and waterproof function at the same time.
