### 2.2. Opis

- ArDura 500 C is lightweight 100% polyamide 6.6 woven fabric for military, police and gendarme wear. It is suitable for mobile motobikers police wear.
- The Pa6.6 high tenacity fibers gives for extra protection for polices.
- The fabric has water and oil repellent function.
- It's used mostly for application which requires ultra durability.
- The antibacterial function is very usefull to eliminate all kind of odor formation during work.
