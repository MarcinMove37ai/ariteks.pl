### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- 80% Polyester 20% Cotton Polycotton twill Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/argiyim-245/images/argiyim-245-main-pic-m__52fb34e3e2.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- 80% Polyester 20% Cotton Polycotton high visible, chemical resistant twill fabric. The front side of the fabric is 100% Polyester which is ultra durable for work conditions and light, the back side of the fabric is cotton which provides breathability to the user.
- ArGiyim²45 are used for outdoor jackets, safety jackets, pans and trousers.
- Certified for EN ISO 20471 and for EN 13034
