## 2. Dane z podstrony produktu

- Nazwa handlowa: **ArDolu**
- Opis z title: `95% Polyester 5% Pu bonded fabric`
- Linia specyfikacji: `210 g/m², 95% Polyester 5% Elastane, 3 layer laminated, Bonded Fabric`

### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- 100% ArAstar plain Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/ardolu/images/ardolu-main-pic-m__996d6d1de6.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- ArDolu is 95% polyester 5% pu bonded fabric. The top layer is woven fabric, the middle layer is breathable tpu membrane, and the last layer is knit fabric.
- The basic usage is for winter parka and raincoats.
- ArDolu is certified for EN 343 and EN 20471
