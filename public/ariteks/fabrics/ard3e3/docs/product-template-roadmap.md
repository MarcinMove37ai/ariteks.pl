### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/ard3e3/images/ard3e3-main-pic-m__87b5d67840.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- Antistatic Satin Fabric 54% Viscose Fr 20% Polyamide 6.6 20% Wool 5% p-aramid 1% Carbon, inherent flame retardant antistatic satin fabric for molten metal splash protection . ArD3E3 fabric is used to make jacket and trousers for hot metal, steel and aluminum industries.
- It is resistant to hot metal splash around 1500'C degrees.
- ArD3E3 are inherent flame retardant with EN 11612 D3 E3 level certification.
- Suitable for antistatic application, certified for EN ISO 1149-5.
- It provides arc flash protection with EN 61482 class 1 (4 kA) certification.
