- Opis z title: `80% Polyester 20% Cotton PolyCotton High Visible Water Repellent Satin Fabric`
- Linia specyfikacji: `290 g/m², 80% Polyester 20% Cotton High Visible, Chemical Resistant, 4/1 Satin Woven Fabric`

### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- 80% Polyester 20% Cotton Polycotton twill Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/argiyim-290/images/argiyim-290-main-pic-m__da8e5ec9d3.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- 80% Polyester 20% Cotton PolyCotton high visible, chemical resistant satin fabric. The front side of the fabric is 100% Polyester which is ultra durable for work conditions and light, the back side of the fabric is cotton which provides breathability to the user.
- ArGiyim²90 are used for heavy outdoor jackets, safety jackets, pans and trousers.
- Certified for EN ISO 20471 and for EN 13034
