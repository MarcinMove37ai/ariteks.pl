  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arwowear-t21-static-pro/images/arwowear-t21-static-pro-main-pic-m__bb32c7d0d4.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- ArWoWear T21 Flame Arc Pro is heavy weight 330 g/m², 79% Cotton 20% Polyester 1% Carbon, vat dyed, antistatic workwear fabric. Due to carbon fibers used on the back side of the fabric it has antistatic certification. (EN 1149-5)
- It is suitable to use on electronic industry where static electric formation is a problem. Oil and gas industry platform workers can also use it to prevent static electric related explosions on dangerous area.
