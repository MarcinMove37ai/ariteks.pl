### 2.2. Opis

- Antistatic Twill Fabric
- ArWoWear T2 Static Pro is 245 g/m², 99% cotton 1% carbon workwear antistatic twill fabric.
- It is certified for EN 1149-5 (EN 1149-3)
- It is suitable to use on electronic industry where static electric formation is a problem.
- Oil and gas industry platform workers can also use it to prevent static electric related explosions on dangerous area.
