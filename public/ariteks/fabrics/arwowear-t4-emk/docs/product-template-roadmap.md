## 2. Dane z podstrony produktu

- Nazwa handlowa: **ArWoWear T4 EMK**

### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- 55% Polyester 25% Cotton 20% Stainless Steel Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arwowear-t4-emk/images/arwowear-t4-emk-main-pic-m__d2d1265a07.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- 55% polyester 25% Cotton 20% Stainless Steel emi shielding twill fabric.
- Arwowear T4 EMK fabrics can be used as emi shileding for walls and baby cloth by reducing all kind of electromagnetic waves.
- Another application is conductive clothing for live line workers, anti-radar applications for military cages, anti-stress and pain reducer bandages.
- Arox T4 Antibac is certfied as IEC 60895 for line workers clothing.
