### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- 50% HPPE 50% Polyester Denim Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/argiyim-denim-kursun/images/argiyim-denim-kursun-main-pic-m__50d635ea3d.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- 50% HPPE (high performance polyethylene) 50% polyester denim twill fabric. The front side of the fabric is polyester which is ultra durable for sun light, the back side of the fabric is hppe which provides extra abraison resistance for motorcyclist's cloth.
- ArGiyim Denim Kursun can be used on abraison resistant cloths.
- EN 388 certified as cut proof.
- EN 17092 certified as motorcyclist norms.
