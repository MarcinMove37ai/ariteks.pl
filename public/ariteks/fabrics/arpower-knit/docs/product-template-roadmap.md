## 2. Dane z podstrony produktu

- Nazwa handlowa: **ArPower Knit**
- Opis z title: `Ultra Elastic Antibacterial Water Repellent Knit Fabric`
- Linia specyfikacji: `400 g/m², 45% Polyester 35% Viscose 22% Polyamide 8% Elastane, Water Repellent, Antibacterial, Elastic Knit Fabric`

### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- Ultra Elastic Knit Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arpower-knit/images/arpower-knit-main-pic-m__be06a35871.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- ArPower Knit is 40% Polyester 30% Viscose 22% Polyamide 8% Elastane, 400 g/m² ultra elastic knit fabric for corset applications.
- It is antibacterial and resistant up to 20 wash.
- ArPower Knit is water and oil repellent, can be used during orthopedical treatments.
- 2 layer of this fabric can be easily used to produce certified EN 14683 face masks.
