### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- 75% Polyester 23% Polyurethane 2% Carbon, Water Resistant, Breathable, High Visible, 1/1 Plain Woven Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arox-t5-multi/images/arox-t5-multi-main-pic-m__ca3481a387.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- 75% Polyester 23% Polyurethane 2% Carbon milky back coated, flame retardant, antistatic, high visible, water repellent, waterproof, breathable oxford fabric.
- Arox T5 Multi fabrics are used for outdoor jackets, trousers and bodywarmers which has waterproof and flame retardant functions. The fabric is inherent flame retardant polyester with antistatic carbon grids.
- Certified for EN ISO 20471, EN 343, EN 1149-5, EN 14116 and EN 13034
