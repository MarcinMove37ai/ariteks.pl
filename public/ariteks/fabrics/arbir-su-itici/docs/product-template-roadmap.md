## 2. Dane z podstrony produktu

- Nazwa handlowa: **ArBir Su İtici**
- Opis z title: `Water Repellent Knit Raincoat Fabric`
- Linia specyfikacji: `140 g/m², 100% Polyester, High Visible, Water and Oil Repellent, Woven Like Knit Fabric`

### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- Flat Surface Warp Knit Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arbir-su-itici/images/arbir-su-itici-main-pic-m__4ac6599232.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- %100 polyester, 140 g/m² woven like warp knit fabric developed for Jackets and Raincoats
- EN ISO 20471 certified.
- Neon dyed fabrics they attract more attention than other colors. They absorb uv light and reflect as a visible light.
- As a result they shine more than other objects.
- Resistant to liquid chemicals. (EN 13034)
