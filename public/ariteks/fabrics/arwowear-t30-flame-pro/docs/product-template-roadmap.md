### 2.2. Opis

- ArWoWear T30 Flame Pro is light weight 260 g/m², 90% cotton 10% polyamide, vat dyed, flame retardant workwear fabric.
- It has flame retardant function with EN ISO 11612 and EN ISO 11611 certification.
- It is durable up to 100 washing.
