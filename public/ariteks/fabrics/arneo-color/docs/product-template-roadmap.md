## 2. Dane z podstrony produktu

- Nazwa handlowa: **ArNeo Color**
- Opis z title: `Fabric for Safety Jackets Non Neon Colors`
- Linia specyfikacji: `120 g/m², 100% Polyester, Warp Knit Fabric`

### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- Warp Knit Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arneo-color/images/arneo-color-main-pic-m__591fe6a119.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- ArNeo Color is a 100% polyester, 120 g/m² warp knitted fabric.
- It used for jackets with reflective stips.
- As its is a jacket with non neon color it has mostly corporate usage than security applications.
