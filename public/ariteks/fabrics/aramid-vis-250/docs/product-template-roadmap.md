### 2.2. Opis

- 250 g/m², 53% Viscose Fr, 40% m-Aramid, 5% p-Aramid, 2% Carbon.
- Inherent flame retardant fabric
- for oil and gas industry workers.
- With viscose fr content is suitable to use in outdoor very hot climates. Inherent flame retardant fabric with EN ISO 11612 certification.
- Antistatic (EN 1149-3)
- Protection against thermal hazards of electrical arc. (EN 61482-1-2)
- Resistant to most industrial chemicals. (EN ISO 13034)
