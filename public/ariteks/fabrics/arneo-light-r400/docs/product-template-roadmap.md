### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- Fluorescent Warp Knit Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arneo-light-r400/images/arneo-light-r400-main-pic-m__691e599527.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- ArNeo Light +R400 is 100% polyester, 65-70 g/m² warp knitted neon colored jacket fabric.
- It is an economic alternative to ArNeo Eco for non professional usage.
- Designed for construction and mining workwear applications.
- The edge of the fabric is laminated with 5 cm EN 20471 certified reflective strips.
