## 2. Dane z podstrony produktu

- Nazwa handlowa: **Arof**
- Opis z title: `Track Suit Fabric`
- Linia specyfikacji: `220 g/m², 100% Polyester, Warp Knit Fabric`

### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- Arof
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arof/images/arof-main-pic-m__64789b3b7c.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- Arof 100% polyester, 220 g/m² warp knitted fabric for track suit with thermal protection function. Backside of the fabric is fleece.
- It is mostly used as sport suits for runners and soldiers.
