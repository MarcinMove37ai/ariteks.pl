## 2. Dane z podstrony produktu

- Nazwa handlowa: **ArGiyim Ic**
- Opis z title: `50% Polyester 50% Cotton PolyCotton High Visible Needle Drop Knit Fabric`
- Linia specyfikacji: `180 g/m², 50% Polyester 50% Cotton High Visible, Needle Drop Knit Fabric`

### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- 50% Polyester 50% Cotton Polycotton knit Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/argiyim-ic/images/argiyim-ic-main-pic-m__82fbde0e27.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- 50% Polyester 50% Cotton PolyCotton high visible, needle drop knit fabric. The front side of the fabric is 100% Polyester which is ultra durable for work conditions and light, the back side of the fabric is cotton which provides breathability to the user.
- ArGiyim İç is used for ultra durable t-shirts.
- Certified for EN ISO 20471
