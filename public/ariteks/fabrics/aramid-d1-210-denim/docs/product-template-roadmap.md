### 2.2. Opis

- 210 g/m², 93% m-Aramid, 5% p-Aramid, 2% Carbon.
- Inherent flame retardant fabric
- for electricity distribution companies and oil and gas industry workers.
- Inherent flame retardant fabric with EN ISO 11612 and EN ISO 11611 certification.
- Antistatic (EN 1149-5) certified.
- Protection against thermal hazards of electrical arc. (EN 61482-1-2)
- Resistant to most industrial chemicals. (EN ISO 13034)
