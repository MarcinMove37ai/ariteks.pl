### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- Flame Retardant Antistatic Fluorescent Warp Knit Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arneo-flame-static-pro/images/arneo-flame-static-pro-main-pic-m__50196a087e.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- Antistatic Knit Fabric ArNeo Pro is 99% polyester 1% carbon, 150 g/m² warp knitted flame retardant, antistatic, neon colored fabric for safety jackets.
- It is mostly used by aeroport apron workers also for oil and gas industries.
- EN ISO 20471 certified.
- Neon dyed fabrics they attract more attention than other colors. They absorb uv light and reflect as a visible light.
- As a result they shine more than other objects.
- Antistatic certified. (EN ISO 1149) (inherent antistatic)
- Flame retardant certified. (TS EN ISO 14116) (resistant to 10 washing)
