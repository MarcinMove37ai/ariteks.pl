### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- Flame Retardant Antistatic Cotton Fabrics
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arwowear-t0-flame-arc-pro/images/arwowear-t0-flame-arc-pro-main-pic-m__51bd60691e.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- ArWoWear T0 Flame Arc Pro is 150 gr/m', 99% cotton 1% Carbon, vat dyed, flame retardant, antistatic shirt fabric.
- It has flame retardant function with EN ISO 11612 certification.
- Due to carbon fibers used on the back side of the fabric it has antistatic certification. (EN 1149-5)
- It is durable up to 100 washing.
