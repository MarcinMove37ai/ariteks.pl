## 2. Dane z podstrony produktu

- Nazwa handlowa: **ArPower Knit P**
- Opis z title: `Ultra Elsatic Antibacterial Water Repellent Knit Fabric`
- Linia specyfikacji: `350 g/m², 70% Polyester 22% Polyamide 8% Elastane, Water Repellent, Antibacterial, Elastic Knit Fabric`

### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- Ultra Elastic Knit Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arpower-knit-p/images/arpower-knit-p-main-pic-m__588fcb17f2.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- ArPower Knit P is 70% polyester 22% Polyamide 8% Elastane, 350 g/m² ultra elastic knit fabric for corset applications.
- It is antibacterial and resistant up to 20 wash.
- ApPower Knit P is water and oil repellent, can be used during orthopedical treatments.
- 2 layer of this fabric can be easily used to produce certified EN 14683 face masks.
