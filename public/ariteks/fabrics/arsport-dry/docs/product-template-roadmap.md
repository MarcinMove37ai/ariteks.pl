## 2. Dane z podstrony produktu

- Nazwa handlowa: **ArSport Dry**
- Opis z title: `Polyester Fast Dry Interlock Knit Fabric`
- Linia specyfikacji: `140 g/m², 100% Polyester Fast Dry, Interlock Knit Fabric`

### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- ArSport Dry, fast dry interlock knit fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arsport-dry/images/arsport-dry-main-pic-m__17ee968b97.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- ArSport Dry is 100% Channeled Polyester interlock knit fabric for team uniforms. The special channeled poliester fiber gives fast dry function to the fabric. To reduce to odor formation the fabric is coated on two side by antibacterial agents.
- It is suitable for sublimation printing.
