  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/ar3iplik-aramid-vis/images/ar3iplik-aramid-vis-main-pic-m__e4f635fa23.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- Antistatic Fleece Knit Fabric 53% Viscose Fr 40% m-Aramid 5% p-Aramid 2% Carbon, inherent flame retardant antistatic fleece knit fabric . Ar3İplik Aramid Vis fabric are mostly used by electrical workers and firefighters.
- With fleece stucture it is suitable to use in cold zones.
- Ar3İplik Aramid Vis is inherent flame retardant with EN 11612 certification.
- Suitable for antistatic applications, certified for EN ISO 1149.
- It provides arc flash protection with EN 61482 class 1 (4 kA) certification.
