## 2. Dane z podstrony produktu

- Nazwa handlowa: **ArNeo Super Eco +R50**
- Opis z title: `Economical Safety Jacket Fabric Laminated with Brillant Strips`
- Linia specyfikacji: `75 g/m², 100% Polyester, Brillant Strips Laminated, High Visible Knit Fabric`

### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- Economical Fluorescent Warp Knit Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arneo-supereco-r50/images/arneo-super-eco-r50-main-pic-m__a54b1b7f8c.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- ArNeo Super Eco +R50 100% polyester, 70-80 g/m² warp knitted neon colored jacket fabric.
- It is suitable for digital printing, thus it is possible to make company's logo printed jackets by sublimation printing.
- Designed for construction and mining workers.
- The edge of the fabric is laminated with 5 cm brillant strips.
