### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- 99% Polyester 1% Carbon Antistatic Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arox-t2-static-pro/images/arox-t2-static-pro-main-pic-m__aace52f894.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- 99% polyester 1% carbon, antistatic ESD workwear twill fabric.
- Arox T2 Static pro fabrics are mostly used for clean room's cloths.
- Suitable for antistatic ESD application like electronic industry, certified for EN ISO 1149-5.
