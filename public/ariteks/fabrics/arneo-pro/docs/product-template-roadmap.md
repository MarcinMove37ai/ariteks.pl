## 2. Dane z podstrony produktu

- Nazwa handlowa: **ArNeo Pro**
- Opis z title: `Flame Retardant, High Visible, Safety Jacket Fabric`
- Linia specyfikacji: `150 g/m², 100% Polyester, High Visible, Flame Retardant Knit Fabric`

### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- Flame Reardant Fluorescent Warp Knit Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arneo-pro/images/arneo-pro-main-pic-m__3f83634b23.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- ArNeo Pro is 100% polyester, 150 g/m² warp knitted flame retardant neon colored fabric for safety jackets.
- It is mostly used by aeroport apron workers.
- EN ISO 20471 certified.
- Neon dyed fabrics they attract more attention than other colors. They absorb uv light and reflect as a visible light.
- As a result they shine more than other objects.
- Flame retardant certified. (TS EN ISO 14116) (resistant to 10 washing)
