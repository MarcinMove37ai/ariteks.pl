### 2.1. Hero / duże zdjęcie

- 60% Modacrylic 38% Cotton 2% Carbon Flame Retardant Twill Fabric
  - Public: `/ariteks/fabrics/arwowear-330-moda-pro/images/arwowear-330-moda-pro-main-pic-m__baa06af21b.jpg`

### 2.2. Opis

- Antistatic 3/1S Twill Fabric 60% modacrylic 38% cotton 2% carbon, inherent flame retardant antistatic twill fabric . ArWoWear 330 Moda Pro fabric are mostly used by welders, electrical workers and firefighters.
- It is resistant to very high sun level, with fastness level of 4 to 5.
- ArWoWear 330 Moda Pro are inherent flame retardant with EN 11612 certification.
- Suitable for antistatic application, certified for EN ISO 1149.
- It provides arc flash protection with EN 61482 class 1 (4 kA) certification.
